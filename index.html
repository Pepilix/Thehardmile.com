Who We Are

A collective of ordinary people doing extraordinary things — for the communities around us.

Endeavour Club is a group of friends who come together each year to take on a physical challenge that pushes the limits of what's possible — and raise money for local charities that need it most. No corporate sponsors, no professional athletes. Just a crew of determined people who believe that with enough grit and enough heart, you can move mountains. Or row 41 miles. Or cycle 500 kilometres. Whatever it takes.
